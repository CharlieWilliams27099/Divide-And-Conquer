🌍 Territory Conquest

Claim land. Build empires. Outplay your rivals.

Made by Charlie · Strategy + Brains + Chaos

🎯 Objective

Conquer land and defeat your rivals by placing tiles, earning gold, and launching attacks.

🧱 Starting the Game

Place 3 starting tiles anywhere on land. Choose one to become your Capital.

🔁 Taking Turns

Each turn, claim land, earn income, train troops, and battle for control.

🧠 Strategy

Protect your capital and expand wisely. Use water to your advantage!
